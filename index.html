About the founder

Josh Molnar (Bitcoin Jae) started Bitcoin Daily in 2017 as a single Instagram page sharing daily market analysis. Before crypto, his first career was music, a signed recording artist and producer who studied audio engineering. He brought that same self-taught discipline to the markets, and Bitcoin Daily grew into one of the largest Bitcoin communities online.

Read the full story at joshmolnar.com.

Our philosophy

  • Education beats signals. If you can't explain the trade, you shouldn't be in it.
  • Risk management is the whole game. Plans written before the trade beat decisions made during it.
  • No hype, no lambos, no guarantees. Trading involves real risk, anyone promising profits is lying to you.
