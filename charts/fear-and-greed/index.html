Source: the Crypto Fear and Greed Index from alternative.me, daily since February 2018. It blends volatility, momentum, social media, and survey inputs into one number.

The Crypto Fear and Greed Index compresses the mood of the market into a single number from 0 to 100. Low readings mean investors are fearful, high readings mean they are greedy. It blends several inputs, including volatility, market momentum, and social media activity, into that one score.

Its value is as a contrarian gauge. The old line that you should be greedy when others are fearful shows up here: extreme fear, under 25, has clustered around cycle bottoms, while extreme greed, over 75, has clustered around tops. Overlaying it on price makes the pattern easy to see.

Sentiment is noisy and can stay fearful or greedy longer than feels reasonable, so it is a context tool, not a trigger. It works best confirming what the valuation and on-chain signals on this site are already saying.
